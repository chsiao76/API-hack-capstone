    Task Attribute Details

    • Activity: Description of the queried activity
    • Participants: The number of people that this activity could involve (0 to N)
    • Type: Category of the activity (education, recreational, social, diy, charity, cooking, relaxation, music, busywork
    • Price: A factor describing the cost of the event with zero being free (0 to 1)
    • Accessibility: A factor describing how possible an event is to do with zero being the most accessible

    Note: Sometimes, attributes for a certain activity aren't available
